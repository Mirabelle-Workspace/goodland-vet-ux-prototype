Dr. Becky Moss

Founder & Veterinarian · Fear Free Certified Professional

Emergency medicine Certified chiropractic care Holistic & integrative options Family-centered care

Dr. Moss completed her undergraduate studies and veterinary degree at UW–Madison, followed by a small-animal rotating and emergency-medicine internship in Cary, North Carolina. Before founding Good Land, she worked at the Animal Emergency Center in Glendale.

She is a certified chiropractic provider with training in holistic and alternative medicine, and brings a flexible approach to treatment, recognizing that there are often many different right paths. She has a special ability to connect with her animal patients and to understand them in the context of their whole family.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Moss is mom to three children and shares her home with two dogs, Pepper and Daisy, and a cat named Maddie. She enjoys soccer, biking, and cooking.
