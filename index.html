Wellness & Prevention

Routine wellness care is the foundation of a long, healthy life. Regular visits let us catch small problems before they become serious and keep your pet's vaccines and prevention up to date.

  • Nose-to-tail physical exams
  • Core and lifestyle vaccinations
  • Parasite prevention (fleas, ticks, heartworm)
  • Nutrition and weight guidance

Most adult pets benefit from a check-up once a year; puppies, kittens, and senior pets are usually seen more often.

Surgical Procedures

From routine spays and neuters to soft-tissue surgery, procedures are performed with careful monitoring, modern pain management, and Fear Free handling before and after.

  • Spays, neuters, and mass removals
  • Soft-tissue surgery
  • Pre-surgical exams and bloodwork
  • Pain management and recovery support

We walk you through preparation and aftercare so you know exactly what to expect.

Specialized Services

Beyond everyday care, we offer treatments that support comfort, mobility, and behavior as part of a whole-pet approach.

  • Cold laser therapy for pain and healing
  • Veterinary spinal manipulation (chiropractic care)
  • Pet behavior counselling

These services can complement traditional medicine when your pet needs a little extra support.

Diagnostic Services

When your pet is unwell, fast, accurate answers matter. Diagnostics help us find the cause and start the right treatment sooner.

  • Bloodwork and lab testing
  • Urinalysis and fecal testing
  • Digital imaging and x-rays

Diagnostics also establish healthy baselines, so we can spot changes early.
