Welcome. We are glad you are considering Good Land for your pet's care. Here is what to expect so your first visit is easy and low-stress.

What to expect on your first visit

  1. A warm, Fear Free welcome designed to keep your pet calm and comfortable.
  2. A thorough exam and a conversation about your pet's history, diet, and any concerns.
  3. A clear, individualized plan, with time for your questions before you leave.

What to bring

  • Any prior medical records or vaccination history
  • A current medication and food list
  • Your questions, written down so nothing is forgotten
