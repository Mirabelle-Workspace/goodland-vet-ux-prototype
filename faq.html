Appointments & New Clients

How do I make an appointment?

Call us at 414-312-4978, email us, or use the appointment-request form on our Contact page. We will confirm a time that works for you.

Are you accepting new clients?

We would love to meet your pet. See our New Clients page for what to expect and what to bring to your first visit. (New-patient status to be confirmed by the clinic.)

What should I bring to my first visit?

Any prior medical and vaccination records, a list of your pet's current medications and food, and any questions you have for the team.

Your Pet's Care

What animals do you treat?

We are a full-service small animal clinic caring for dogs and cats.

What does "Fear Free" mean?

Our team is Fear Free Certified, which means we use gentle, low-stress techniques to keep your pet calm and comfortable throughout the visit.

Prescriptions & Refills

How do I request a refill?

Order through our Online Pharmacy or Purina Vet Direct (see the Prescriptions page). Prescription items are reviewed and approved, which may take 1 to 2 business days.

Can my pet's medication be delivered?

Yes, both ordering options ship directly to your home. (Typical delivery timelines to be confirmed by the clinic.)
