# Good Land Veterinary Clinic Website Audit

**Website:** Good Land Veterinary Clinic - goodlandvetclinic.com

## Executive Summary

The website is clean, professional, easy to navigate, and communicates the clinic's values well. Its strongest aspects are the personal introductions from Dr. Moss and Dr. Kelly, the clear local-ownership message, and the simple navigation.

The biggest opportunities are:
- Stronger appointment conversion
- Expanded service content
- More trust-building and educational content
- Accessibility improvements
- Local SEO improvements
- Better support for new clients

## Homepage

### What Works Well
- **Clear positioning:** Visitors immediately understand who the clinic is, where it is, what animals are treated, and that it is locally owned.
- **Strong doctor introduction:** Featuring Dr. Moss and Dr. Kelly up front builds trust and personality that many veterinary sites bury deeper.
- **Contact info is easy to find:** Phone, address, hours, and email are all visible.

### Opportunities (High Priority)
- **Add a "Request an Appointment" CTA:** The current primary CTA, "Get In Touch," is vague.  
  Consider: Request an Appointment, Schedule Appointment, Become a New Client. Action-focused CTAs outperform generic contact CTAs because visitors know exactly what to do.
- **Add client testimonials:** The homepage has no reviews or testimonials.  
  Consider: a short reviews section that builds trust before a visitor makes contact.
- **Add a "Why Choose Good Land" section:** The clinic's story is a differentiator and deserves emphasis.  
  Consider: locally women owned, independent practice, personalized care, experienced veterinarians, Fear Free approach.

## Our Team Page

### What Works Well
- **The doctors are the clinic's biggest asset:** Clients choose veterinarians they trust, and the page creates a genuine personal connection.

### Opportunities
- **Add more professional photos:** Helps future clients picture the experience.  
  Consider: team photos, clinic photos, behind-the-scenes images.
- **Introduce the full team and surface credentials:** Only the two doctors appear today.  
  Consider: support staff and technicians (names, roles, photos), plus credentials and special interests on the team page.

## Services Page

### Highest Opportunity on Entire Website
- **Add dedicated, detailed service content:** Use either expandable detail per service or dedicated pages.  
  Examples: Wellness Exams, Vaccinations, Dentistry, Surgery, Senior Pet Care, Laser Therapy, Diagnostics, Behavior Counseling.
- **Benefits:** better SEO, better client education, more trust, better conversion.
- **Add FAQ sections:** Helps both clients and SEO.  
  Consider: per-service FAQs, for example under Dental Care:  
  - How often should my pet have a cleaning?  
  - What are signs of dental disease?  
  - What happens during the procedure?

## Prescriptions Page

### What Works Well
- **Online ordering is strong:** The Online Pharmacy and Purina Vet Direct integrations let clients order medications, diets, and food for home delivery, and the page is simple to understand.

### Opportunities
- **Add trust messaging and refill guidance.**  
  Consider: why purchase through our pharmacy, manufacturer guarantees, prescription verification, home-delivery convenience, how long refills take, how refills are approved, and when to call the clinic instead.

## Get In Touch Page

### What Works Well
- **The essentials are present:** Address, phone, and email are all here. (Hours and an embedded map currently appear on the homepage.)

### Opportunities
- **Add a New Client section:** Likely to provide significant value.  
  Consider: what to expect at the first visit, forms to complete, vaccination records needed, payment information.
- **Strengthen the contact and conversion path on this page.**  
  Consider: an appointment-request form, tap-to-call on mobile, and hours plus an embedded Google Map on the contact page itself (directions, parking, nearby landmarks).

## Accessibility Review

Recommend auditing:
- Keyboard navigation
- Focus indicators
- Form labels
- Alternative text
- Heading hierarchy
- Color contrast

## SEO Opportunities

- **Service pages:** Currently the largest SEO opportunity.
- **Educational content:** Potential topics include puppy vaccines, kitten wellness, senior pet care, dental disease, pet obesity, and when to call the vet.
- **Local SEO:** Expand content referencing likely search areas: Fox Point, Glendale, Bayside, Whitefish Bay, and Milwaukee North Shore.

## Questions

To prioritize recommendations and spot opportunities not visible from the public site:
- What platform is the website built on?
- Who currently maintains it?
- Is there a content management system?
- Is there access to Google Analytics?
- Is there access to Google Search Console?
- Are appointment requests tracked?
- Are there any frustrations with updating the website today?
