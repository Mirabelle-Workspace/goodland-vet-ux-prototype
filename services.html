Wellness Exams & Vaccinations

A wellness exam is a head-to-tail check of your pet's overall health, paired with the vaccines that protect against common, serious diseases.

  • Full physical: heart, lungs, eyes, ears, skin, joints, and teeth
  • Core vaccines plus lifestyle vaccines based on your pet's needs
  • Time to discuss diet, behavior, and any concerns

Recommended yearly for most adults, and more often for puppies, kittens, and senior pets.

Preventative Medicine

Preventing illness is easier, safer, and less costly than treating it. Prevention keeps parasites, dental disease, and chronic conditions in check.

  • Flea, tick, and heartworm prevention
  • Dental care and home-care guidance
  • Nutrition and weight management
  • Baseline bloodwork to track changes over time

Diagnostics

Diagnostic testing gives us the information we need to make confident decisions about your pet's care.

  • In-house and reference-lab bloodwork
  • Urinalysis and fecal testing
  • Digital x-ray and imaging

Results often guide treatment the same day.

Soft-Tissue Surgery

Soft-tissue surgery covers procedures that don't involve bones, from routine to more complex.

  • Spays and neuters
  • Mass and lump removals
  • Wound repair and other soft-tissue procedures

Every surgery includes anesthetic monitoring, pain control, and clear aftercare instructions.

Dental Procedures

Dental health affects the whole body. Professional cleanings and treatment protect your pet's teeth, gums, and comfort.

  • Cleaning and polishing under safe anesthesia
  • Dental x-rays to check below the gum line
  • Extractions when a tooth can't be saved

Watch for warning signs like bad breath, drooling, or trouble eating.

Cold Laser Therapy

Cold (low-level) laser therapy is a gentle, drug-free treatment that uses light to reduce pain and inflammation and support healing.

  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Wounds and post-surgical recovery
  • Soft-tissue injuries

Sessions are quick and painless, and most pets relax during treatment.

Veterinary Spinal Manipulation

Veterinary spinal manipulation (animal chiropractic) supports mobility and comfort by addressing the spine and joints.

  • Stiffness or reduced mobility
  • Recovery support after injury
  • Part of a whole-pet wellness plan

Provided by a certified practitioner as a complement to standard care.

Pet Behaviour Counselling

Behavior is health, too. Counselling helps with anxiety, training challenges, and unwanted behaviors using low-stress, Fear Free methods.

  • Anxiety, fear, and stress
  • House-training and basic training concerns
  • Changes in behavior that may signal a medical issue

Early help often prevents small issues from becoming big ones.
