Dr. Marta Kelly

Founder & Veterinarian · Fear Free Certified Professional

Soft-tissue surgery Preventative care Client education

Dr. Kelly earned both her undergraduate and veterinary degrees from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Her background includes wildlife conservation research with an emphasis on reptile populations, and she now focuses on companion-animal medicine for families across the Milwaukee area.

Her special interests include soft-tissue surgery, preventative care, and educating clients so they can make confident decisions for their pets.

Born in Poland and raised in the Chicago suburbs, Dr. Kelly is married with three children and shares her home with two cats, Scout and Toulouse. She enjoys hiking, puzzles, and napping.
